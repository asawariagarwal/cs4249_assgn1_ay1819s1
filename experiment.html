This experiment is designed to compare the performance of marking and radial menus. Please follow the instructiins given below to participant in the experiment:


  • Please fill in your Student ID in the field below before starting the experiement.
  • On the separate pre questionnaire provided to you, please fill out your student ID as well as other rewuired details,
  • Once you begin the experiment, you will see a table on top describing the experiemnt condition i.e. type of menu, depth of menu etc. as well as the target item to be selected
  • Using the conditions as a guide, please select the taregt value from the given menu
  • For the marking menu to appear you will need to press mouse button and scroll down. Once it appears, to select an item just drag your curson to the appropriate value
  • For the Radial menu to appear, please press the right mouse button. Once it appears, just left click the appropriate menu item
  • After making the selection, please clink on the next button. You can make multiple attempts in the same experiemnt condition before cliking the next button. Each attempt will be recorded.
  • You can check the procgress of the experiemnt on the leftmost cell in the table
  • Once the experiemnt is finished, please clink on the Done button and wait for it to download the results
  • Please do not forget to fill out the post experiemnt questionnaire handed over to you.
